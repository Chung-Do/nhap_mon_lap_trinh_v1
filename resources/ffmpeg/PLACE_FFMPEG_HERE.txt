DE BUNDLE FFMPEG VAO APP (KHONG CAN DOWNLOAD MOI LAN CHAY):
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

1. Download ffmpeg cho Windows:
   → https://www.gyan.dev/ffmpeg/builds/
   → Chon file: "ffmpeg-release-essentials.zip" (~70MB)

2. Giai nen file ZIP va tim "ffmpeg.exe" trong thu muc "bin/"

3. Copy "ffmpeg.exe" vao thu muc nay (resources/ffmpeg/)

4. Chay lai build script:
   → build_server.bat

5. Done! FFmpeg da duoc bundle vao app.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

✓ Chuc nang webcam/video hoat dong ngay lap tuc
